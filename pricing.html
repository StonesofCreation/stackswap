Common Questions

Pricing FAQ.

Is the Pro Audit a subscription?

No. It's a one-time $99 purchase. You receive the report, own it, and there is no renewal, no recurring charge, and no upsell call.

Why is Intelligence $999/year and not less?

The product is market intelligence, not a SaaS tool. $999/year is priced against the value of the data — not against feature count. It's positioned as a research and decision-making resource, not a productivity add-on.

Does any vendor pay to be recommended?

No. No vendor pays for placement, recommendation, or ranking at any tier. The business model is operator subscriptions, not vendor advertising. This is structural, not a policy.

When does Intelligence launch?

Intelligence opens when the dataset volume justifies the product. We're collecting submissions now. Join the waitlist on the Intelligence page to be notified at launch.

What is Enterprise pricing?

Enterprise is custom — typically $7,000–$25,000/year depending on use case. It includes API access, white-label reporting, and custom data exports. Contact us to discuss.

Can I run the free analysis before deciding?

Yes. The free analysis is the first step regardless of which tier you end up on. Start there — if it surfaces enough to warrant a deeper look, the Pro Audit is the next step.

Need custom data access?

Enterprise licensing covers investors tracking portfolio adoption trends, consultants benchmarking client stacks, and ops teams needing white-label reporting. Custom contracts, API access, and dedicated support.
