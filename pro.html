FAQ

Common questions.

What's the difference between the free report and the Pro Audit?+
The free report flags overlaps and gives a summary. The Pro Audit is a decision document — full tool-by-tool breakdown, line-level savings projections, a prioritized action plan, and an industry-specific ideal stack blueprint. It's built to be shared with a team and acted on immediately.
How does StackSwap generate recommendations?+
Recommendations are generated by pattern-matching your stack against 50+ overlap rules, an AI-native swap knowledge base, and industry-specific blueprint logic. No vendor pays for a recommendation. If a tool appears in the audit, it's because the data supports it.
Are the savings estimates accurate?+
Savings figures are estimates based on current vendor list pricing. They do not account for negotiated enterprise rates, annual prepay discounts, or multi-seat tiers you may already have. Treat them as directional guidance, not exact figures.
Is this a recurring subscription?+
No. The Pro Audit is a one-time $99 purchase. You receive the report and own it. There is no renewal, no subscription, and no upsell call.
What industries does StackSwap support?+
StackSwap has industry-specific blueprint logic for SaaS/Tech, Real Estate Brokerage, Real Estate Team, Marketing Agency, E-commerce, Finance/Fintech, Healthcare, Legal, and 11 additional categories. The analysis adapts based on the industry you select.
